Game/Film Scoring

I'm a long time fan of movies, orchestral music, and all elements that add up to an epic soundscape. I've been playing for over 15 years and have developed a musical style that incorporates tension and beauty in a way that can elevate visual media. I will develop a sonic aesthetic for your project, creating a soundtrack to complement the narrative progression of your work. Ranging from short incidental background music, to a fully developed soundtrack based on your requirements. I'm looking for challenging concepts and people who are really invested in their projects.



Sound Design

I'll make your project come to life with brilliant sound effects and professional mixing. Simply send me your video and other materials (such as music and voiceovers), write a description of what you would like and enjoy an excellent finished product. Each project is unique and takes a different time to complete.



Mixing & Mastering

Looking for a professional and experienced engineer to fine tune and polish your production? Your song doesn't play well on all playback systems? You want your vocals to sound professional? I'm here to help. I've recorded, produced, mixed and mastered various projects in rock, bedroom pop, singer/songwriter, indie, acoustic and more! I use premium and industry standard plugins to achieve high quality results that'll get your songs ready for release. Each project that I work on, I treat with the same attention to detail as I do for my own music. It's important to me that you are absolutely satisfied with the end result. Multiple revisions are free.



Private Lessons

We'll work together to personalize your lesson(s) and come up with a unique curriculum catered to help you achieve your goals. Whether they're as simple as just learning to play your favorite song(s) and/or a cool riff/solo or as complex as learning new genres and advanced techniques. I also specialize in Songwriting Techniques, Music Theory, and Music Composition. If you're more interested in just learning how to write songs/music, we can focus on that without the need to go over technique. Or we can combine it all!
